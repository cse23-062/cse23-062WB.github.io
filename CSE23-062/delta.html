Delta

Delta Game" is a strategic board game designed for two players, each controlling a fleet of spaceships. The objective is to strategically position and maneuver your ships to eliminate your opponent's fleet while protecting your own. Players take turns moving their ships across a grid, using various abilities and tactics to outmaneuver and outsmart their opponent. The game offers a blend of skill, strategy, and anticipation, making each match unique and engaging.

Game Information

  • Developer(s): Stavros Fasoulas
  • Publisher(s): Thalamus Ltd
  • Composer(s): Rob Hubbard
  • Platform(s): Commodore 64, ZX Spectrum
  • Release: February 1987
  • Genre(s): Scrolling shooter
  • Mode(s): Single-player, two-player
