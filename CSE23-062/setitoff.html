Set it Off

"Set It Off" is a 1996 crime thriller directed by F. Gary Gray. The film follows the story of four close friends from Los Angeles who are struggling with financial hardships and decide to rob banks to improve their lives. Each woman has her own motivations and reasons for participating in the heists, but as they become more successful, they also attract more attention from law enforcement. As the stakes rise and tensions mount, their friendship is tested, and they must confront the consequences of their actions. "Set It Off" explores themes of loyalty, desperation, and the pursuit of the American Dream against the backdrop of urban life in the 1990s.

  • Directed by: F. Gary Gray
  • Screenplay by: Takashi Bufford, Kate Lanier
  • Story by: Takashi Bufford
  • Produced by: Oren Koules, Dale Pollock
  • Starring:
    • Jada Pinkett
    • Queen Latifah
    • Vivica A. Fox
    • John C. McGinley
    • Kimberly Elise
